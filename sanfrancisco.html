San Francisco

San Francisco is a city that I love dearly. It was a place I could only see on TV when I was a little kid living in a small town. I could hardly believe that, in my early 20s, I found out I was living just an hour away from it. The city vibes, the views, the beach, the forest, the hikes, and the memories I've created with my people during my visits have warmed my heart every time I think about it. It feels like watching a movie and wishing to be a main character in it. And somehow you did! San Francisco truly is one of my dreams come true.
In fact, it reminds me that the American dream is no longer just a fantasy. Dreams really do come true. All I need to do is keep dreaming, even when my aunt says I dream too much. But hey, San Francisco is the place where I can simply romanticize my life, and I would never turn down an opportunity to visit this city. Sometimes, I wonder if one day I could live or retire here. Maybe…Keep dreaming!
