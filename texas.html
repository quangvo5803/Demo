Road trip Texas - New Mexico - Colorado 2019

I love road trips, and this was my very first one in the U.S., starting from Amarillo, Texas, to Pagoda, Colorado, with a stopover in Santa Fe, New Mexico, alongside my favorite person on Earth—CMCC. Since then, I've taken quite a few road trips, but I'll never forget the unique feelings my very first journey brought me. It's safe to say she inspired my new favorite hobby: traveling, specifically road-tripping. I cherish the feeling of freedom on the road, where you can go anywhere, stop anytime, and simply admire the beauty around you. Being able to travel wherever you want is such a privilege.
