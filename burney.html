Burney Trip 2025

One of the most significant things I've learned from this trip is about personal interests that no one should judge or comment on. For example, we might be fascinated by visiting Lake Shasta Cave, while a child might only be interested in playing with a small pond of water inside that same cave. Yet age plays an important role too, and over time, those interests can change. However, the idea of respecting others' interests should remain constant—let's not judge, but rather honor those interests! :) p/s: I'm not really a waterfall person; they're just too strong for my personality. However, I will always admire the strength and passion they embody.

I believe there is extraordinary beauty in the simplest of moments.
And that beautiful moments deserve awesome photographs.
